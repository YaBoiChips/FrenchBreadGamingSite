French Bread Gaming

Edmonton Based, Canadian focused.

French Bread Gaming runs weekly meetups and larger tournaments focusing on Super Smash Brothers and other various fighting game community. We build local pro scenes, help new players grow, and bring top talent to Alberta. Our mission is to make High Level fighting game eSports fun and accessible in Western Canada
