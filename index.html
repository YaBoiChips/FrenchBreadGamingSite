French Bread Gaming

Edmonton Based, Canadian focused.

French Bread Gaming runs weekly tournaments and larger events focusing on Super Smash Brothers and other various fighting games. We build local pro scenes, help new players grow, and bring top talent to Alberta. Our mission is to make High Level fighting game eSports fun and accessible in Western Canada and bring Edmonton closer to becoming an eSports Hub!
